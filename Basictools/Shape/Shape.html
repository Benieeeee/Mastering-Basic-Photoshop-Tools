SHAPE TOOL


The photoshop hand tool is used to be able to view parts of an image especially when you’re zoomed in. The tool allows you to navigate and pan through parts of an image by selecting it on the toolbar or activating it by holding on “space” as its shortcut on your keyboard. Alternatively, for prolonged use, you may also press the “h” on your keyboard to permanently select it. After that, you may simply select an area on your canvas and drag the tool along. The icon on the toolbar looks like a hand.
